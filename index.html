About Al-Hanoof

Al Hanouf Electromechanical Services Co. Ltd is a Libya-based company headquartered in Tripoli, specializing in delivering integrated electromechanical solutions across the energy sector. Since its establishment in 2018, the company has built a strong presence in the local market through the execution and contribution to numerous key energy projects, particularly in collaboration with the General Electricity Company of Libya and its affiliated companies.

The company is committed to delivering high-quality services in line with international standards, with a strong focus on safety, reliability, and operational efficiency, serving both public and private sector clients in the fields of electricity, oil, and gas.
